What is BurnUI?

BurnUI is a game creator / a game player. BurnUI has been in production since 2022 by the name of "Retrostudio" and has been released in early 2026. BurnUI is ran by two people, Myles, and Matthew.

How do I know when to update BurnUI?

You can go to the BurnUI YouTube Channel ( @BurnUI-Official ) for all the videos to watch without needing BurnUI or go to the posts and see if your game is outdated.
